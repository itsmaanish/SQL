### ✴️ What is PEMDAS?

PEMDAS defines the order of operations in expressions.

- **P** – Parentheses  
- **E** – Exponents *(not common in SQL)*  
- **MD** – Multiplication and Division *(left to right)*  
- **AS** – Addition and Subtraction *(left to right)*

**Example**:  
```sql
(age + 10) * 10  
-- If age = 35 → (35 + 10) * 10 = 450
